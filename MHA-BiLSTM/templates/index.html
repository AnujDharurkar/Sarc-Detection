Our network uses Bidirectional LSTM with the multi-head mechanism. Multi-head attention allows the model to jointly attend to information from different rep- resentation subspaces at different positions.This Multi-Head Attention- based Bidirectional LSTM (MHA-BiLSTM), consists of five main parts.

MHA-BiLSTM is implemented and accordingly the accuracy and detection of sarcasm is notified to the user. Additon of features to the existing model will enhance the performance even more. Implementation of the Discord BOT shows its potential to be integrated in automated chat service centers.
